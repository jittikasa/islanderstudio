## Learnings & Best Practices

### What Worked Well

1. **Systematic Planning**: Clear acceptance criteria prevented scope creep
2. **Pillar Content Strategy**: 3,000-word comparison guide provides long-term SEO value
3. **Fair Comparisons**: Acknowledging competitor strengths builds trust
4. **Research-Backed Content**: Citing Dr. Phillippa Lally, James Clear, BJ Fogg adds authority
5. **Natural Keyword Integration**: Keywords woven into narrative, not forced
6. **Metaphor-Driven Content**: "Pearls" metaphor makes technical features memorable

### Areas for Optimization

1. **Keyword Density**: Some posts at 1.2% (target: 2.5%)
2. **Internal Linking**: Not yet implemented (planned)
3. **FAQ Schema**: Designed but not yet deployed
4. **Image Optimization**: Alt text templates created, screenshots needed
5. **A/B Testing**: Headline variations created but not tested yet

### Process Improvements for Next Sprint

1. **Batch Content Creation**: Group similar tasks (all planning together, all writing together)
2. **Template Library**: Create content templates for faster production
3. **Screenshot Bank**: Pre-create app screenshots for all content
4. **SEO Checklist**: Automated pre-publish SEO validation
5. **User Testimonials**: Collect and integrate social proof
